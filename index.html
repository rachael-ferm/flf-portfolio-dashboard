Methodology — Execution Score + 2026 Trending
Year 1 Score = 0.60·Rev Attainment + 0.25·GM Attainment + 0.15·Burn Attainment. Attainment = Actual ÷ EOY Plan, capped 1.5× / floor 0. Burn-overage penalty only when Rev Attainment < 1.0. Missing components auto-renormalize.
Year 1 Plan — 2025 EOY projections from Fund 3 Catch Up sheet (deal-close projections). Permanent baseline, never overwritten.
Year 1 Actual — Q4 2025 annual revenue from Airtable · Financial Metrics · field Annual Revenue.

Year 2 Velocity Panel tracks three layers for 2026:
  ① BAP — Board-approved plan (primary benchmark). Enter inline below; most important number per Saxon — formally committed, updated at year-end. Replaces Catch Up as the execution benchmark for 2026.
  ② Forecast Trend — Q4 2025 founder EOY forecast → Q1 2026 founder EOY forecast. Rising = company gaining confidence. Cutting = flag.
  ③ Catch Up Plan — original investment-case projection. Reference only (muted). Everyone lives at deal close.

Tiers: ≥ 1.10 🟢 Ahead · 0.85–1.09 🟡 On Track · 0.70–0.84 🟠 Moderate Miss · < 0.70 🔴 Significant Miss · ⚪ Pending.
